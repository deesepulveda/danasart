Meet the Artist!

Dana

Tuy

I am a Deaf Artist and Graphic Designer specializing in custom canvas and original digital art. I specifically work with acrylics and adobe illustrator to create personalized pieces for clients. My passion for drawing began at 3, and I fell in love with painting and digital art in high school. Since then, I have been sharing my experiences on Instagram, TikTok, and Youtube as a deaf artist navigating the hearing world. Feel free to message me to learn more about my work and custom pieces. I would love to chat with you!
